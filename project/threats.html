Threats to Insects

Some threats to insect species include pollution, urbanization, invasive species, pesticide, monoculture, and light pollution.

How you can help

Plant native species in your yard, replace lawn grass with native grass species, use companion plainting instead of pesticide, use light fixtures that direct lighting downwards and prevent it from lighting the sky, vote locally and reach out to your senators to implement local policies against light pollution.
